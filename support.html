Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my account?

To verify your account, please go to your account settings and upload clear copies of your ID document (national ID, passport, or driver's license) and a recent utility bill or bank statement not older than 3 months. Verification usually takes 24-48 hours.

What payment methods can I use?

We accept M-Pesa, Airtel Money, bank transfers, and several mobile payment options. You can view all available payment methods in the cashier section of your account. Minimum deposit is KES 100 and maximum varies by method.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times vary by method: M-Pesa withdrawals are usually instant but can take up to 2 hours during peak times. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days. All withdrawals are subject to security checks which may delay processing.

Why was my bonus not credited?

If your bonus wasn't credited automatically, please check that you: 1) Met all qualifying criteria, 2) Used the correct bonus code if required, 3) Didn't already claim this bonus type before. If everything checks out, contact support with details of your qualifying transaction.

How do I set deposit limits?

You can set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits in the Responsible Gaming section of your account settings. Limits take effect immediately and can only be decreased or removed after a 24-hour cooling-off period. Increasing limits requires a 7-day waiting period.
